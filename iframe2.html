WELCOME TO CMR UNIVERSITY

Overview

CMR University (CMRU) is a private university in the state of Karnataka, established and governed by the CMR University Act-2013. CMR University aims to promote and undertake the advancement of university education in technical, health, management, life sciences and other allied sectors of higher and professional education.

Mission

To offer multi, inter and cross-disciplinary modular programmes with technology-enabled teaching-learning processes. To focus on research-led teaching and learning in an innovative and interdisciplinary learning environment; to create critical thinkers. To create leaders for knowledge based economy, with ethical demands of a society base. To engage talented intellectual capital with strong faculty diversity in knowledge and experience. To ensure transformation of learning into positive behavior of students.
